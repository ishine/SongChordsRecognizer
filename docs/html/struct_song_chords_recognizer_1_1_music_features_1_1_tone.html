Detailed Description

Structure of tones. We have 108 tones (9 octaves and 12 chroma). One Tone is characterized by Description (e.g. C, C#, ...), Octave (e.g. 0 - sub contra, 1 - contra, .., 8 - five lined), Chroma (instances of Chroma - C, C#, ..., B) Frequency (default accurate frequency of that Tone), L_FreqBorder, R_FreqBorder (borders of deviation from the Frequency value)
